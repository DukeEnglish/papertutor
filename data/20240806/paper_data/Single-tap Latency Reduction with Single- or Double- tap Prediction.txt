Previousworkshaveinvestigatedend-to-endlatencyperceptionindifferentformfactors(i.e.,

Furthermore,theend-to-endlatencyintapping(singletaps)onadouble-tappablecomponent

levelcanbeconfiguredbytheuserfromtheaccessibilitysettings.Specifically,thedefaultsettings
