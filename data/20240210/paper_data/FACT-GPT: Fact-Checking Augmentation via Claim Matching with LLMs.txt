3.1 TaskDescription
ToevaluatevariousLargeLanguageModels‚Äô(LLMs)performanceinclaimmatching,weemployatextualentailment

Entailment,Neutral,andContradiction.ApairisclassifiedasEntailment iftheveracityofStatementAinherently
impliesthetruthofStatementB.ThepairislabeledasNeutralifthetruthfulnessofstatementAdoesn‚Äôtaffirmor
denystatementB‚Äôstruth.It‚ÄôsidentifiedasContradictionifStatementA‚ÄôstruthinfersthatStatementBisfalse.Textual

ofmisinformation[8,23].However,it‚Äôschallengingduetothescarcityofcontradictionpairsinreal-worldinstances
